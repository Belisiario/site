Services

Your site built with state of the art design process:

01

Desk Research

With the use of data already available, this phase aims to explore the topic and become familiar with the business environment.

02

Benchmarking

Metrics will be elaborated in order to compare services or products, by direct and indirect competitors.

03

Information Architecture

Site Map is the list of pages within a website and the relationship between them. Wireframe is the visual guide that represents the structure of the site. Prototyping is an early version of the final product used to evaluate the design.

04

Design System

A collection of reusable components of a website or app created to improve the consistency of the brand across all products and increase the productivity.

05

Development

The website will be developed in Webflow and transferred to you after the final, approved version, along with the files HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
