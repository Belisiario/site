Travel Article

The elaboration of this article considered the whole experience the reader should have. The design and the elements in it were thoroughly applied using the travel subject as the foundation.

Creation Process #1

The layout composition of an article may also beneficiate from a data-driven background. That is, whereas an academic subject is more prone to have a rigid structure, an undemanding, fun travel post will have its creative side preponderant.  ​

In this case, the predefined layout was not a requirement, but designing a few options at the beginning could be helpful in the development of the article.

Creation Process #2

There's always room for improvement and new ideas. After a process of iteration with the font choices, colors, and whole gamut of the title, this was the semi-finished page 1.

Creation Process #3

With a few more improvements it was achieved a more compatible and creative way to display the subject. That is a mini representation of the thought process that occurs in every design project.

The Final Project

In addition to the compatible visual representation of the subject presented, the final result may lead the reader to the social media or the website mentioned just after the article, resulting in a more engaged public.

Article about Stock Market

Considering the article's subject: "Stock Market Investment," and the specific target reader, the design displays a somber, minimalist, and high-end aspect to match the persona's attributes.

Although the objective is to showcase the article in a magazine, it can also be adept into an infographic, website, or post page.
